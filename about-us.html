Welcome to Go Play Indoor Turf

Where we offer the ultimate sports experience in Kerala!

We take pride in being the first in Kerala to offer not just one, but two indoor turfs on one land. Our facility boasts one of the best indoor turfs in Kochi, featuring the tallest height for an unmatched playing experience.

Conveniently located near Infopark Phase 2, our turfs are easily accessible for everyone.

    At Go Play Indoor Turf, we provide a variety of activities to suit your interests:

  • Indoor Cricket

  • Indoor Football

  • Cricket-Football Coaching

  • Yoga and Meditation Sessions

  • Event Hosting

    • Our space is versatile and perfect for all your sports and wellness needs.
